About Us

The lot, the family, and the man behind it all.

The Story

Larry started this lot with one idea.

In 1970, Larry opened the doors at 228 W Newport Pike with a small inventory, a handshake policy, and the belief that buying a used car didn't have to feel like a battle. It still doesn't.

Over 60 years later, the lot looks mostly the same. So does the way Larry does business — straightforward pricing, no games, and a genuine interest in making sure you drive away in something you'll be happy with.

Larry's has served generations of Newport families. First-time buyers, parents getting their kid's first car, people who've been coming back for decades. The lot has stayed because it does right by people.

Larry is still here most days. Stop in and say hello.
